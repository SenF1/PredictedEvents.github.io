1992 February 28, 2022 2022 Shanghai COVID-19 outbreak Hito Ai A Japanese TV show called 《Wonder Zone》conducted an experiment on future predictions by hypnotizing a person. The show hypnotized the hostess named Hito Ai. After she was hypnotized, the hypnotist said to her, "You go 30 years future." This show was in 1992, and in 30 years it will be 2022. She closed her eyes and said, "This is Tokyo? Why is there no one on the street? It feels like the city is running, why is there no one on the street?" The host said, "Is it really Tokyo?" She said, "It just feels like a big city, and I'm not sure if it's Tokyo." At that time, they predicted that there might be a massive earthquake 30 years later in Tokyo, but this speculation was overturned at that time. Even if there is a major earthquake in Tokyo, the city will not be empty, only the city will be functioning. This prediction is now believed to be Shanghai, China. A COVID-19 outbreak in the city of Shanghai, China began on February 28, 2022, and ended on August 7, 2022. The outbreak was caused by the Omicron variant and became the most widespread in Shanghai since the pandemic began two years prior.
